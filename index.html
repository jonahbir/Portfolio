About Me

Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Yonas Birhanu and i have graduated grade 12 from ODA Boarding School. I am an Ethiopian with diverse interests, always eager to learn and try new things. Over the years, I have developed a strong passion for technology, creativity, and leadership, shaping my journey to where I am today. I enjoy web development, app creation, video editing, digital illustration, as well as 3D modeling and product design. I have received certificates from AddisCoder (one of Africa’s most rigorous CS camps), Moot Court, and STEM Power—for which I am very grateful. I am also deeply proud of writing a book in Afan Oromo, my native language. It is to inspire Ethiopian youth in their mother tongue. I believe in lifelong learning, giving back to my community, and making an impact through technology and innovation.
